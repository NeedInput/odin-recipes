Butterscotch Scrolls

Description

A delicious recipe for Butterscotch scrolls. Biddie is an expert at making this recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups SR flour
  • 20g butter
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 40g butter
  • 3 tbs brown sugar

Steps:

  1. Collect all ingredients.
  2. Set oven at 200℃.
  3. Rub 20g butter into flour using fingertips.
  4. Make a well in the centre and add the milk.
  5. Mix with a spatula and mix into a soft dough.
  6. Turn out onto a lightly floured bench and knead until smooth.
  7. Cream 40g butter with brown sugar.
  8. Roll out into an oblong 30cm * 20cm and spread with creamed mix.
  9. Roll up and seal edges. Cut into 12 slices.
  10. Arrange on paper lined oven tray and glaze with milk.
  11. Bake at 200℃ for 15-20 mins or until golden brown.
  12. Carefully remove with a spatula and place on a cake cooler.
